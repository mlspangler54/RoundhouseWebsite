Train Revolution: Roundhouse

A backlog of workorders at the local roundhouse has left the railyard in disarray! It is up to your team of dispatchers to sort the train cars to complete workorders and get the trains moving on time. Cooperate with your fellow rail workers to move train cars about the roundhouse to complete work orders of unique train car patterns in this puzzle-solving game. Be forewarned, 5-years of obstacle management experience will be necessary. CHOO CHOO!
